Delete Your BoomTAP Account and Data

Welcome to BoomTAP by Huseynoff Inc.

If you wish to request deletion of your BoomTAP account and all associated personal data stored in our Firebase database, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at boomtap.inc@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My BoomTAP Account".
  2. Include your registered email address or username in the email for verification purposes.
  3. We will process your request and permanently delete your account and all related data within 5 business days.

What Data is Deleted

Upon deletion, all your personal data stored in Firebase related to your BoomTAP account will be permanently removed.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 30 days for app performance analysis, but it will not contain any personal identifiers.
